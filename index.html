A Brief Biography of Tony Soprano

Tony Soprano is a complicated New Jersey resident. On the outside, he is a hot-tempered Waste Management consultant, but behind that facade he is the leader of the most powerful Mafia family in New Jersey. He secretly loves animals, and always wishes he had the makings of a varsity athlete.

Tony's Work Life

Tony, or Skipper among other names to his crew, is the leader of the DiMeo Crime Family. After taking over for his Uncle Junior, Tony oversaw and ran numerous schemes for his family, including drug trafficking, retail theft, embezzlement, among many other serious crimes.

Tony's Soft Side

Tony is not your typical mob boss. As a family man, and someone who suffers from panic attacks, he may be the only Don to regularly attend therapy sessions. Doctor Jennifer Melfi talks him through his struggles running the both of his different families and helps him try to find balance in his life.
